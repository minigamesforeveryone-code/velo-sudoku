# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

Velo Sudoku collects only the minimum amount of information necessary to show advertising, analyze usage to improve the game, and keep essential game statistics.

## 2. Advertising Services (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to serve ads. Your advertising data is processed according to your preferences and consent.

### Non-Personalized Ads

When non-personalized ads are selected, Google delivers contextual ads using:
* Coarse location.
* Content keywords.
* General device information only.

**No ad personalization profile is built for you inside the app.**

### Personalized Ads

If you enable personalized ads, Google AdMob may use your advertising ID and activity across other apps to tailor content. You can change this decision at any time from the dedicated advertising consent screen within the app's settings.

## 3. Analytics Services (Firebase Analytics)

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users engage with the game and to improve the overall experience.

### Collected Data
This service collects anonymous usage data, which may include:
* Session duration and engagement.
* Gameplay events (e.g., levels completed, tools used).
* General device information.

**Analytics data collection adheres to your choices made via Google Consent Mode.**

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

* **No Personal Servers:** We do not store any personal data on our own servers.
* **Local Storage:** Advertising preferences, consent status, and local game statistics are saved **only on your device** in encrypted shared preferences.

## 5. Diagnostics and Error Reporting

For error diagnostics, the app may collect anonymous crash reports that include:
* Device model and Android version.
* Stack trace of the error.

**These reports never contain gameplay data nor any information that directly identifies you.**
